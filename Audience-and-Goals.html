Audience and Goals

This portion of the process book discusses what my audience is, and also what my goals are.

Audience

Interested in Art

UCAC has a lot of flexibility in regards to demographic. There is a large focus on younger people, with the evidence being that there is "Kids Corner" tab specifically set apart for childrens events.

Supporting Art

These people can fall into the category of being interested as well, but realistically they will help fund UCAC.

Both Interested in Art

Again, this category is extremely broad, but it is what the commmunity will offer realistically.

Goals

Why does this site need to exist?

To alert members and non-members of upcoming events made available to the community, and to donate.

Are there any specfic metrics that me or my client are shooting for?

More traffic on the site, and more donors through the traffic.

What will make you look at this site as a success?

Overall feeling of professionalism while still maintaining the personality that the original site tried to obtain.

Site Goals

What is the sites impact?

To spread awareness of UCAC's art culture

What is the sites format?

The site will be a service format: to organize, represent, and remind.

How would a visitor be prompted?

Seeing others do it, and if they are reminded.

Where would a visitor visit the site?

They would most likely do it at home.

Overarching Goals

Client (UCAC)

Raise awareness and participate in events.

Gain donations to fund itself.

Partner with other Union community organizations (schools and churches).

Audience

Get information on UCAC events

Give money and resources to UCAC

Connect with Union community through UCAC

Website

Be a vehicle for users to quickly be notified of upcoming events

Let users be able to donate easily
