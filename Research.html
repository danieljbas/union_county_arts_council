Primary Research

Union County Arts Council Website

The Union County Arts Council is an organization that essentially is location driven, and uses their space for galleries, classes, and other fine art events for the local county. Their about page is very small, and only states mainly that "UCAC sponsors many events throughout the year that help enrich the arts experience of the residents of Union and surrounding counties."

Secondary Research

Stories of Interest

Davis Arts Center

Davis Art Center was a unique find, because what caught my attention was an article related to how they responded to COVID. Their solution was to pack art kits for their campers, and have a zoom session where friends could meet with one another, and work on art.

Other Solutions

What Didn't Work

Community Art Center

I could have included many bad examples, but this one clearly showed the issue I kept seeing: overall uninviting, and very unclear. Many links went to the same area, and as a user, I felt stuck.

What Worked Very Well

Art House Charlotte

What inspired me here from this website was the simple grid layout presented, making the elements visually easy to read. Another element was the limitations of two colors for the background and headings, etc.

Ogden Art Center

This one is my favorite and best result. I found this from a Google Ad, not even my search result. There is a lot to unpack. The branding works very well, from the color scheme to the typeface choice. The layout is simple yet very informative, making it easy to nagivate.
