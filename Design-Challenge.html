Design Challenge

The design questions that follow were taken from IDEOs "Field Guide to Human Centered Design"

What is the problem you are trying to solve?

Bettering the user experience and organization for an art centers website.

Make it a question.

How might I better the user experience and organization for an art centers website?

What is the ultimate impact?

For the community to feel more united through the UCAC website, and for there to be familiarity.

What are some possible solutions?

The website should have an inviting atmosphere, clear color palette, branding, hierarchy, and layout.

What are some possible constraints?

A lot of advertisements and tabs have a different design that are not as unified as they can be.

Make a New Question!

How might UCACs website be even more unified and community friendly to invite more users from the Union County area?
